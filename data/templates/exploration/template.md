# Problem Statement

What are you exploring? Include:
- The question or hypothesis
- Why this exploration matters
- Current understanding and gaps
- Expected outcomes

## User Perspective

Who benefits from this exploration?
- Stakeholders interested in findings
- Teams that will use the results
- Impact on user experience

## Engineering Framing

Exploration approach:
- Research methodology
- Technical spikes or prototypes
- Constraints and boundaries
- Non-goals (what we're NOT exploring)

## Validation Thinking

How will you know the exploration is complete?
- Definition of done
- Decision criteria
- Documentation requirements
- Next steps based on findings
